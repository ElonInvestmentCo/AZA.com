Redesign the website to use a bright, premium white interface instead of the current dark theme.

The goal is to create a clean, modern fintech experience that immediately feels trustworthy, premium, fast and easy to use.

DESIGN DIRECTION

• Replace the dark background with a clean white background throughout the website.
• Use generous white space to make every section feel open and premium.
• Keep the PayVora brand colors as accent colors instead of making the interface dark.
• Use black or dark charcoal text for readability.
• Use very light gray (#F7F8FA or similar) for section backgrounds where contrast is needed.
• Add soft shadows instead of dark borders.
• Use subtle rounded corners (14–20px) consistently.
