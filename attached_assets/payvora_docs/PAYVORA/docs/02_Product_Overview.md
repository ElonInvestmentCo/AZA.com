# Product Overview

## Introduction

PAYVORA is a unified financial super app designed to consolidate payments, digital assets, and everyday financial services into a single ecosystem. It enables users to manage money, transact globally, and access essential services from one secure platform.

The platform removes fragmentation in fintech by combining wallets, trading systems, and bill payment infrastructure into one seamless experience.

---

## Core Features

### Wallet

- Multi-currency balance storage
- Instant peer-to-peer transfers
- Transaction history and analytics
- Secure authentication and encryption

---

### Crypto Trading

- Buy and sell major cryptocurrencies
- Real-time price feeds
- Portfolio tracking
- Instant conversion between fiat and crypto

---

### Gift Card Trading

- Purchase and redeem digital gift cards
- Marketplace-based availability
- Secure code delivery system
- Brand-specific catalog

---

### Virtual Cards

- Instant virtual debit card generation
- Online global payment support
- Spending limits and controls
- Card freeze and regeneration options

---

### Airtime

- Mobile airtime top-ups
- Multi-carrier support
- Instant delivery
- Saved contacts for quick recharge

---

### Data Bundles

- Mobile data purchase across carriers
- Prepaid and postpaid support
- Auto-renew options
- Usage optimization suggestions

---

### Electricity

- Utility bill payments
- Meter/account validation
- Instant receipt generation
- Payment tracking

---

### Cable TV

- Subscription and renewal management
- Provider integration
- Auto-pay support
- Subscription history tracking

---

### Betting Funding

- Wallet-to-betting account funding
- Provider integrations
- Transaction monitoring
- Responsible usage controls

---

### Live Rates

- Real-time FX rates
- Cryptocurrency market prices
- Conversion calculator
- Historical rate trends

## User Types

- Retail Users: everyday financial consumers
- Freelancers: cross-border earners and gig workers
- SMB Owners: business transaction handlers
- Crypto Users: traders and investors
- Emerging Market Users: mobile-first financial adopters

---

## User Journey

1. Onboarding with email/phone verification
2. Identity verification (KYC)
3. Wallet creation and funding
4. Daily transactions (payments, transfers, bills)
5. Advanced usage (crypto, cards, trading tools)

---

## Navigation Structure

- Home Dashboard (wallet overview)
- Pay (airtime, data, bills, cable)
- Trade (crypto, gift cards, rates)
- Cards (virtual card management)
- Activity (transactions and history)
- Profile (settings, security, KYC)
