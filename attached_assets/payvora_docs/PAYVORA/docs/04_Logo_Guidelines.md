# Logo Usage

## Primary Logo

The PAYVORA primary logo is the core brand mark used across all major product surfaces. It combines a geometric symbol representing financial flow with a clean wordmark for clarity and trust.

- Used in main app headers
- Marketing and investor materials
- Website and landing pages
- Official documentation

---

## Secondary Logo

The secondary logo is a simplified variant of the primary mark.

- Symbol-only version for compact spaces
- Wordmark-only version for minimal layouts
- Used in UI headers, footers, and constrained layouts

---

## App Icon

The app icon is a simplified geometric representation of the PAYVORA brand.

- Must remain recognizable at 16px
- No text included
- Centered, balanced composition
- Optimized for mobile home screens (iOS/Android)

---

## Favicon

The favicon is a micro-optimized version of the app icon.

- 16x16 and 32x32 pixel formats
- Single-color or high-contrast version
- Must maintain clarity at smallest scale

---

## Wordmark

The PAYVORA wordmark is a modern sans-serif typographic treatment.

- Clean geometric sans-serif font
- Custom kerning for balance
- Can be used alone or with symbol
- Avoid decorative styling

---

## Clear Space

To preserve visual integrity, maintain consistent spacing around the logo.

- Minimum clear space = height of the “P” in PAYVORA
- No elements (text, icons, or graphics) within this area
- Applies to all logo variations

---

## Minimum Size

- Primary logo: 120px width minimum
- Symbol only: 24px minimum
- App icon: optimized for 16px visibility
- Never scale below readability thresholds

---

## Incorrect Usage

The PAYVORA logo must not be altered.

- Do NOT stretch or distort
- Do NOT rotate or skew
- Do NOT apply gradients or shadows
- Do NOT change colors outside approved palette
- Do NOT place on low-contrast backgrounds

---

## Background Rules

- Prefer solid neutral or brand-approved backgrounds
- Ensure high contrast at all times
- Avoid busy or noisy imagery behind logo
- Use dark logo on light backgrounds and vice versa

---

## Color Variations

Approved logo color versions:

- Full color (primary blue + neutral tones)
- Monochrome black
- Monochrome white

Restrictions:

- No unauthorized gradients
- No neon or experimental color shifts
- Maintain strict brand consistency

---

## Export Formats

- SVG (primary scalable format)
- PNG (transparent backgrounds)
- WebP (optimized web usage)
- PDF (print and investor decks)

---

## SVG Guidelines

- Must be vector-clean and resolution independent
- Use minimal paths and nodes
- Avoid unnecessary grouping layers
- Ensure scalability across all sizes
- Optimize file size for performance
